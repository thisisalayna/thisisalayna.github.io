Machine Learning with Wekinator


Using the Wekinator application, a leap motion, and a continuous drum output, I created a certain response when the user positions their hand flat upwards, flat downwards, in a fist, and at a diagonal. The more you train the Wekinator to respond to in a specific fashion, the harder it can be for it to interpret the original actions you wanted it to display.
